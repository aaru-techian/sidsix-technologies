About Us

SIDSIX is a non-profit, open-source platform committed to empowering human minds with the skills needed to thrive in an AI-driven world. What began as a simple idea to create meaningful educational tools has evolved into a collaborative hub where developers, educators, and innovators unite to build engaging apps that ignite curiosity, foster critical thinking, and prepare the next generation to meet the challenges of tomorrow.

Join Our Mission

Collaborate with Us

We're inviting educators, developers, designers, and dreamers to contribute ideas and co-create meaningful learning tools.

Freelancer Opportunities

Are you a freelance developer? Join us to contribute part-time on impactful open-source projects focused on children’s education and future-ready skills.

Internships

College students are welcome to intern with us. Gain hands-on experience, collaborate with a purpose-driven team, and build a portfolio while making a real impact.

Inplant Training

We offer structured inplant training programs for engineering and IT students. Learn real-world tools and practices by working on live educational app development projects.

Project Work

Final-year students can collaborate with SIDSIX to complete academic project work that goes beyond theory — applying skills to build open-source tools that shape future learning.

On-Demand Training

Need a custom training module on emerging tech, app development, or AI fundamentals? We provide flexible, on-demand training sessions tailored for institutions and individuals.
